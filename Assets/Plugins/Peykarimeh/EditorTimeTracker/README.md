# ⏱️ Editor Time Tracker for Unity

**Boost your productivity and keep track of your development hours directly inside the Unity Editor.**

Editor Time Tracker is a modern, feature-rich, and user-friendly tool designed to help Unity developers manage their time effectively. Whether you're a solo developer tracking hours for a client, managing project workloads, or just want to maintain a healthy work-life balance with focus breaks, this tool has you covered.

![Tracker UI](Documentation/Images/ui_preview.png)

## ✨ Quick Overview

*   **📊 Automatic Time Tracking:** Tracks active development time while the Unity Editor is open and active.
*   **🎯 Smart Goals:** Set Fixed Daily goals or Dynamic Weekly goals to track your progress visually.
*   **💤 Idle Detection (AFK):** Automatically pauses when you step away and asks if the time away was work-related when you return.
*   **⏰ Focus Breaks:** Integrated Pomodoro-style reminders to help you take regular breaks to avoid burnout.
*   **🎨 Customization:** Multiple UI Themes and a Minimalist view option to blend perfectly with your workspace.
*   **💾 Robust & Safe:** Survives script recompilations, auto-saves reliably, and supports seamless CSV spreadsheet exporting.

## 🚀 Quick Start

1.  **Open the Tracker:** Navigate to `Tools` > `Project Time Tracker` in the top menu of the Unity Editor.
2.  **Dock It:** We recommend docking the window next to your Inspector or Project view.
3.  **Start Working:** The timer starts automatically as you interact with the editor.
4.  **Configure:** Expand the **⚙ SETTINGS & TOOLS** foldout at the bottom to personalize goals, themes, and break reminders.
